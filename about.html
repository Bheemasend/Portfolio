Goals

As a Web developer, I want to be able to design Web pages that can effectively display material, provide interactivity, and be artistically pleasing to the user. I need to know the nuances of Web design and delivery and the various tools and software applications..

Goals

1 - High Quality Theme

It is important that any website theme chosen is professional looking and user-friendly as possible. Some themes will allow you to tweak some aspects such as modifying the colour scheme, images and layout. This will allow the website to match the style preferred by the company and in so doing have a site which will attract visitors and hopefully customers and clients.

2 - Customer Satisfaction

The first impression is the last impression. Did you know that over 75% of users make purchasing decisions based on colors? This means more than half of Internet users will not even bother to check your app or website if they don’t find the color appealing.Therefore, I need to take careful while building the user experience of your app/website.I need to research what element your users like and what they don’t, and then design the user experience accordingly.

3 - Well Mentained Sites

The best website in the world is absolutely no good to anyone if it constantly crashes, is too slow or if it is offline for any length of time. Should that happen customers are very likely to visit a competitor’s website and in turn purchase what they have on offer.The key to avoiding these problems is to for any business to ensure its website is kept well maintained.
